Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026 · Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lummo ("we," "our," or "us"). Lummo is a mobile application designed to help you track, record, and analyze your sleep patterns and dreams. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store and protect it, and what rights you have regarding your data. By using Lummo, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This policy applies to the Lummo mobile application available on Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password (or authentication tokens if you sign in via a third-party provider such as Apple or Google).
  • Dream Journal Entries: Text descriptions of your dreams, tags, mood ratings, lucidity indicators, and any other content you choose to record.
  • Sleep Logs: Sleep and wake times, sleep quality ratings, and related notes you manually enter.
  • User Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and personalization choices.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • App Usage Analytics: Screen views, feature usage frequency, session duration, and interaction patterns (collected in anonymized or pseudonymized form).
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, language settings, and time zone.
  • Crash and Performance Data: Crash logs, error reports, and performance metrics to help us identify and fix issues.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect health data from Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect unless you explicitly enable such integration and grant permission.
  • We do not access your device's microphone, camera, or contacts without your explicit consent.
  • We do not collect precise geolocation data.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by reputable infrastructure providers. Dream journal entries and sleep logs may also be cached locally on your device for offline access.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256 or equivalent).
  • Secure authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the service. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following categories of third-party services:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve the user experience (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar). These services may collect anonymized usage data and device identifiers.
  • Crash Reporting: To detect and resolve technical issues (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, or similar).
  • Cloud Infrastructure: To securely store and process data (e.g., Google Cloud Platform, Amazon Web Services, or similar).
  • Authentication Providers: If you choose to sign in with Apple, Google, or another third-party provider, we receive limited profile information as permitted by you.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review those policies. We do not share your dream journal content or sleep log details with third-party analytics or advertising services.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — EEA/UK Residents

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Request that we limit processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (e.g., JSON or CSV export).
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@lummo.app.

We will respond to your request within 30 days. If we need additional time, we will notify you.

6.2 Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) — California Residents

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information, so this right does not apply.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

6.3 Data Export

You may export your dream journal entries and sleep logs at any time through the app's settings. Exported data is provided in a standard format (e.g., JSON or CSV).

7. Children's Privacy

Lummo is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at support@lummo.app.

8. International Data Transfers

If you access Lummo from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be transferred across international borders. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, to protect your data in compliance with applicable law.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
  • Notify you through the app or via email (if you have provided one).

Your continued use of Lummo after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
